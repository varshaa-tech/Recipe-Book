About Us

A Family Run Local Favourite in Fairyland

We are an independent, family run, neighbourhood restaurant. We are passionate about sustainably sourced seafood and our aim is to deliver the highest quality food and service in a relaxed, welcoming yet vibrant environment.

We showcase sustainable seafood mostly sourced from the British Isles with dishes that are designed to be shared. Oysters are served either classically or dressed with specific garnish to compliment the texture and flavours of the individual oysters.
